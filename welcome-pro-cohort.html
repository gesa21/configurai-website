How the Professional Cohort works

Six weeks, structured so you can fit it around real life and a real inbox.

  • A new video module is released every Monday. Modules build on each other. Watch them in order.
  • Videos are self-paced. Each module is roughly one to one and a half hours. Watch whenever works for you during the week.
  • Every Wednesday, join the live group call. 8:00 PM UK time.
  • Live calls are where you ask questions, get feedback, and troubleshoot. They run for as long as we need. Bring real work from your role, not theoretical questions.
  • All calls are recorded. If you cannot make the live call, the replay is available within 24 hours.

The Programme

Your six modules

Built for senior professionals using AI inside their role. Every module ends with something you have built, not just learned.

1

Foundations

Configure ChatGPT and Claude for your specific role. Learn the RCTC prompting framework. By the end of week one, your AI knows who you are, what you do, and how to help.

2

Daily Workflows

Build an email system that writes in your voice. Meeting prep, faster reports, spreadsheet analysis in plain English. Target: 3 to 5 hours saved this week.

3

Your AI Employees

Build Custom GPTs pre-loaded with your role and documents. Set up automations for daily digests, meeting notes, and weekly reports. Target: 5 to 8 cumulative hours saved.

4

AI as Your Coach

Stress-test decisions with Devil's Advocate mode. Rewrite your CV or LinkedIn profile. Build a personal Life Coach GPT. AI moves from work tool to thinking partner.

5

Governance and Responsible Use

Data privacy, GDPR, and where human judgement must lead. Quality control habits. The advanced playbook: Voice Mode, Deep Research, and image analysis used safely.

6

Putting It Into Practice

Your Before and After results showcase. A 90-day growth plan. Community access for the long run. You leave with a documented number of hours saved every week.
