The Process

How It Works

Step One

Initial Conversation

A 30 minute call to understand your team's size, the platforms they use, the skills gap you are trying to close, and the outcomes you need to demonstrate to the business. No obligation, no sales deck.

Step Two

Bespoke Programme Design

A training programme designed around your team's actual workflows, the tools they already have, and the outcomes agreed in the first conversation. Delivered as a written proposal with clear scope and pricing.

Step Three

Delivery and Measurement

Training delivered live, either in person or remotely, with follow-up support and a documented record of outcomes. Measurable results agreed before we begin, reported at the end.
