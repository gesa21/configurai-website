The Approach

How I Teach

I teach the way I wish someone had taught me - plainly, practically, and without jargon. I focus on two tools: ChatGPT and Claude. These are the tools that professionals, consultants, coaches, and small business owners are using every single day, and they are good at different things. You will learn both, know when to use which, and understand why.

Once you know how to use these two tools properly - how to communicate with them, how to set them up for your role, and how to spot when they are making things up - you can apply the same skills to Gemini, Grok, or whatever comes next. Master the principles. The rest will follow.

The Programme

Six Weeks. Six Modules.

Everything is built around your actual job. No generic examples. Your work becomes the curriculum.

Pre-Course

AI Audit

Three days before we begin, you complete a short form about your job, your biggest time drains, and what you have tried with AI so far. This shapes the entire course. No generic examples. Your work becomes the curriculum.

Week One

Set Up AI Properly and Learn to Communicate With It

Configure ChatGPT for your specific role. Learn the RCTC prompting framework - the difference between getting a useless answer and getting something you would actually send to a client. Learn to spot when AI is making things up (it happens more than you think). By the end of this week, your ChatGPT will no longer be a blank page. It knows who you are, what you do, and how to help.

Week Two

AI for Your Daily Work

Build an email system that writes in your voice. Create meeting prep workflows. Draft reports in 30 minutes instead of 3 hours. Upload a spreadsheet and get plain-English insights in 60 seconds. This is the week where the time savings become real. Target: 3 to 5 hours saved.

Week Three

Your AI Employees

Build Custom GPTs pre-loaded with your role and documents. Set up automations that run without you - daily digests, meeting notes, and weekly reports. These are not theoretical exercises. You will build them using your actual job. Target: 5 to 8 cumulative hours saved per week.

Week Four

AI as Your Business, Career and Life Coach

Stress-test a business decision with AI's Devil's Advocate mode. Rewrite your CV or LinkedIn profile in 5 minutes. Build a personal Life Coach GPT. This is where AI stops being a work tool and becomes a thinking partner.

Week Five

The Advanced Playbook

Voice Mode for walking meetings. Photograph anything for instant analysis. Deep Research for structured reports in 3 minutes. Create presentations in 5 minutes. After this week, you know more about AI than anyone in your office.

Week Six

Where Not to Use AI - and Your System for Life

Data privacy and GDPR. Where human judgement must lead. Quality control habits. What AI is genuinely bad at. Your Before and After results showcase. Access to the ConfigurAI community for ongoing support.
