The Full Assessment

What Is Included

Every element is built around your role. Nothing is generic. The report is written specifically for the way you work.

01

Live Zoom Interview

30 to 45 minutes. We cover your role, your typical week, the tasks that drain the most time, and what you have already tried with AI. No preparation required.

02

Personalised Diagnostic Report

7 to 9 pages written specifically around your role and workflows. Delivered within 48 hours of the interview. Not a template with your name dropped in - a document built for you.

03

Follow-Up Debrief Call

30 minutes to walk through the report findings, answer questions, and discuss the implementation steps that will have the biggest impact first.

04

Four Prioritised Recommendations

Each recommendation includes full implementation detail - what to do, what tool to use, and how long it will take to set up. Ordered by time saved per week.

05

ROI Calculation

A concrete calculation showing hours recovered and cost saved per week based on your role and salary range. A number you can take to a manager or use to justify your own time investment.
