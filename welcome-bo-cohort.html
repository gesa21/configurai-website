How the Business Owner Cohort works

Six weeks, structured so you can fit it around running a business.

  • A new video module is released every Monday. Modules build on each other. Watch them in order.
  • Videos are self-paced. Each module is roughly one to one and a half hours. Watch whenever works for you during the week, between client calls and team meetings.
  • Every Tuesday, join the live group call. 11:00 AM UK time.
  • Live calls are where you ask questions, get feedback, and troubleshoot. They run for as long as we need. Bring real problems from your business, not theoretical questions.
  • All calls are recorded. If you cannot make the live call, the replay is available within 24 hours.

The Programme

Your six modules

Built for owners running businesses with roughly 5 to 50 employees. Every module ends with something you have built into the business, not just learned.

1

Foundations

Configure ChatGPT and Claude around your business, your voice, and your offers. Learn the RCTC prompting framework. By the end of week one, your AI knows your company, your customers, and how you sound.

2

Daily Workflows

The everyday admin that eats your week. Email drafting in your voice, meeting prep, faster reports, spreadsheet analysis in plain English. The first hours saved come back this week.

3

AI Systems for Your Business

Client intake automation. Call automation and transcription. Chatbots that handle real questions. Proposal generation that does not feel templated. Content systems for social media and newsletters that keep running while you sleep.

4

AI for Business Growth

Lead acquisition. Cold outreach that does not read like a template. Sales call preparation. Website and landing page creation. Content as a growth tool. The commercial side of your business, with AI doing the heavy lifting.

5

Governance and Responsible Use

Data privacy, GDPR, and where human judgement must lead. What you can let your team use, and how. Quality control habits so AI does not embarrass the brand you have built.

6

Putting It Into Practice

Your Before and After results showcase. A 90-day rollout plan for the rest of your team. Community access for the long run. You leave with a measurable shift in what the business can do without adding headcount.
