The Person Behind the Programme

Orgesa Meli

Seventeen years in corporate finance. Three businesses. A mum of two. A competitive public speaker. And the person who built ConfigurAI from inside her own working week.

The Background

17 years in corporate finance. Budgets, financial models, and back-to-back meetings where every number had to be right. I was good at it. I ran things. But I was building someone else's business, and I knew it.

Why I Built This

This is the programme I wish someone had designed for me. Every AI training I found was either too technical, too theoretical, or taught by someone who had never had a real job with real deadlines and a real inbox. I wanted something a finance director could take on a Wednesday evening and use on Thursday morning. So I made it.

Why AI

I came to AI the way you probably did. Opened ChatGPT. Typed something. Got a mediocre answer. Nearly closed it and moved on. But I do not close things until I have properly tried them. I spent months testing every tool, every prompting technique, every workflow. I figured out what actually saves time and what just looks impressive on LinkedIn. Now I use AI to run three businesses, content, training, communications, and research, and I still pick my children up from school at half three.

I figured out what actually saves time and what just looks impressive on LinkedIn.

The Change

In my late 30s, with two small children, I left. No tech background. No investors. No safety net. Just a decision I could not take back and did not want to. In the same year, I rebuilt my health from the ground up, not with a coach or a programme, just with the same stubbornness that got me through seventeen years of finance. I proved to myself I could change something I thought was permanent. That mattered more than any job title I ever held.
