Honest about the fit

For you if

  • You have a job and you want AI to actually help you do it.
  • You have tried AI, found it underwhelming, and want to see what it looks like when it works.
  • You want practical prompts and workflows, not theory.

Why I built this

I run ConfigurAI alongside 3 other businesses. AI saves me hours every week, and the only reason it does is because I treat it like a tool I had to learn properly, not magic.

AI is overwhelming right now. Too noisy. New tools every week, subscriptions you forget you are paying for, prompts that work in demos but not in your real job. Most of what is published is hype or theory.

This community is what I would have wanted to join two years ago. One place where I show you which updates are worth your time and which to ignore, the tools that actually fit into your day, prompts applied to your real work rather than generic templates, and workflows built around your specific job so you stop guessing whether AI can help with X and start knowing exactly how.

That is it.
