The Diagnostic Areas

What We Look At

The assessment covers four operational areas. Every recommendation is rooted in at least one of them.

01

Client Systems

Intake, onboarding, follow-up, and communication. Where is time lost between a lead expressing interest and a client getting started? What is being done manually that should not be?

02

Operations and Team

Recurring tasks, internal communications, reporting, and team workflows. What is being done by people - including you - that AI can do without them?

03

Content and Growth

Social media, newsletters, proposals, and outreach. How much time is going into content? Is it generating clients? What should be systematised?
