Is This You?

Who This Programme Is For

You run a business - a consultancy, a service firm, a coaching practice, or a small team - and you are doing far too much of it yourself.

You have heard that AI can help, but every time you try something, it feels like a parlour trick rather than a business tool. Generic outputs. Prompts that take longer to write than the task itself.

You want AI systems that actually run parts of your business without you - client intake, content, research, proposals - so you can spend your time on the work that only you can do.

And you want to understand the privacy and ethical questions too. Not just what AI can do, but where it should not go near your business.

The Approach

How I Teach

I teach the way I wish someone had taught me - plainly, practically, and without jargon. I focus on two tools: ChatGPT and Claude. These are the tools that professionals, consultants, coaches, and small business owners are using every single day, and they are good at different things. You will learn both, know when to use which, and understand why.

Once you know how to use these two tools properly - how to communicate with them, how to set them up for your business, and how to spot when they are making things up - you can apply the same skills to Gemini, Grok, or whatever comes next. Master the principles. The rest will follow.

The Programme

Six Weeks. Six Modules.

Everything is built around your actual business. No generic examples. Your operations become the curriculum.

Pre-Course

AI Audit

Three days before we begin, you complete a short form about your business, your biggest operational drains, and what you have tried with AI so far. This shapes the entire course. No generic examples. Your business becomes the curriculum.

Week One

Set Up AI Properly and Learn to Communicate With It

Configure ChatGPT for your business. Learn the RCTC prompting framework - the difference between getting a useless answer and getting something you would send to a client. Learn to spot when AI is making things up. By the end of this week, your ChatGPT knows what your business does, who your clients are, and how to help you.

Week Two

AI for Your Daily Business Operations

Build an email system that writes in your voice. Create client communication workflows. Draft proposals in 30 minutes instead of 3 hours. Upload data and get plain-English insights in 60 seconds. This is the week where the time savings become real. Target: 3 to 5 hours saved.

Week Three

AI Systems for Your Business

This module shifts from personal productivity to business infrastructure. You stop building tools for yourself and start building tools your entire business runs on. Custom GPTs and Claude Projects configured around your specific services, your client language, and your team's recurring tasks. Client intake automations - forms, onboarding details, follow-up sequences. Call automation - transcription, action points, and follow-up emails generated automatically. Chatbots handling client enquiries and first-contact responses without you. Proposal generation - building first drafts from a brief in minutes. Content systems - social media, newsletters, and recurring content built around your voice.

Week Four

AI for Business Growth

Lead acquisition - finding the right prospects without spending hours on LinkedIn manually. Cold outreach - emails that do not read like templates because they are not. Sales call preparation - walking into every conversation knowing exactly what the client needs before they tell you. Website and landing page creation - building or rebuilding pages with AI in hours rather than weeks. Content as a growth tool - using what you publish to bring clients to you rather than chasing them.

Week Five

The Advanced Playbook

Voice Mode for client calls and walking meetings. Photograph anything for instant analysis. Deep Research for competitor intelligence and market reports in 3 minutes. Create presentations and pitch decks in 5 minutes. After this week, you know more about AI than most of your competitors.

Week Six

Where Not to Use AI - and Your System for Life

Data privacy and GDPR for your business. Where human judgement must lead - especially with clients. Quality control habits. What AI is genuinely bad at. Your Before and After results showcase. Access to the ConfigurAI community for ongoing support.
